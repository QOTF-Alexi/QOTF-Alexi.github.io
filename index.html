Favourite trains 'n stuff?

I do have some, yeah. I've only been in Dutch ones, though, so that's all I know about. RET metro: MG2/1 (aka Type B or 5300 series) and SG2/1 (aka Type S or 5400 series). They're basically the same). Number 5337 has a special place in my heart, since it always has an unoccupied spot for me, no matter how crowded it is.

NS trains: ICNG (aka Wesp). It's fast and it's comfortable. And it makes super interesting noises when accelerating. I'm also fond of the DDZ and VIRM, as they're very comfortable, and (no match for the ICNG or ICM) the top floor gives you amazing views of the landscape you're travelling through.

R-Net (which is basically NS but red) has a nice sprinter too (the Stadler FLIRT 3). It's quiet, but still makes interesting sounds.
As you can tell, I am very much into the sounds that different trains make.
